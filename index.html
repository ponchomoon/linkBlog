Reflect on this phase and the project that I have completed.

Blog Link: Phase 0 Portfolio Project


  1. What went well? Key Learning: The key to learning in web development is perseverance, discipline and self-motivation. it requires doing examples every day, coding and learning new commands. I have learned the process of searching blogs and pages for the answers to the errors that have appeared in each of my attempts to create a web page.

  2. What could have been improved? We know that true learning is not about memorizing. What's more, most of what is memorized is lost in a matter of hours. To retain what they have been taught, students must assimilate the content by constructing meanings in different ways, and making use of our own skills; in other words, we must encourage them to be the protagonists of their learning process. This is why I need to apply the concepts learned to new pages and try to improve the exercises previously carried out.

  3. The key take aways:
    1. Drink water: This liquid is vital for your learning, if you are a woman it is recommended that you drink about 2.7 liters and for men, it is about 3.7 liters; 20% of water consumption occurs through food.
    2. Exercise the body: By doing a little exercise we produce endorphins and hormones, we also increase neurotransmitters, all this allows us to be in a good mood, have good energy levels, and we can be more attentive; This translates into better learning ability.
    3. Sleep:Lack of sleep reduces the ability to react to stressful situations, you can become frustrated faster than you reason, and even the easiest problems can be difficult to solve.
    4. Stress reduction strategies:By doing activities that are to your liking, resting a little, surrounding yourself with people you appreciate, as well as expressing your feelings, they help to lower your stress levels, do not hesitate to apply them.

  4. The process you followed?: my learning process in web development has been approximately four months. When I started I only followed internet tutorials without understanding what I was doing but I did not give up, at this point in the process I already have commands and basic concepts clear in my mind. I am studying a boot code with Academy Xi and this blog is part of one of my activities.
